About Us

Located in the Greater Toronto Area, where we have been serving our clients for over 12 years. Our company is dedicated to providing the highest quality stone products and services to meet all your needs. We specialize in custom fabrication of natural and engineered stone products such as countertops and fireplaces.
Our team of experienced professionals takes pride in their work, ensuring that every project is completed with precision and attention to detail. We use state-of-the-art technology and the latest fabrication techniques to create stunning and durable stone products that will enhance the beauty and functionality of any space.

Stonica Group has a reputation for excellence in the industry, thanks to our extensive experience and dedication to quality. Our team includes highly skilled craftsmen who have years of experience working with various types of stone, including granite, marble, quartz, and more. We take great care to ensure that every piece of stone we work with is carefully selected and inspected for quality and durability.
We pride ourselves on providing a comprehensive range of services to meet all of your stone fabrication needs. Whether you are looking to create a custom countertop, fireplace surround, or other stone feature for your home or business, we can help.
